Batman: Under the Red Hood

There is a mysterious vigilante in Gotham City by the name of the "Red Hood" who kills criminals in their own sense of justice, and goes face-to-face to Batman. Where past and buried memories of the Batman are brought to the light

The Characters/Cast of Batman: Under the Red Hood

    Batman (Bruce Greenwood)
    Bruce Greenwood was born on August 12, 1956.

    Known for being an actor/voice actor and playing roles like Pike in Star Trek (2009) and Thirtheen Days as John F. Kennedy, etc.

    Batman came to be when Bruce Wayne's parents died in a crime alley by a criminal which in most stories was Joe Chill after watching a movie/show in a theater and going to that alley as a shortcut but it resulted in a tragedy and the birth of the Batman. Bruce Wayne as the Batman would fight crime and bring justice to them by sending them to Jail with a moral code of never killing.
    Red Hood (Jensen Ackles)
    Jensen Ackles was born on March 1, 1978.

    Known for being an actor/voice actor and playing roles like playing Soldier Boy in The Boys, Playing as Dean Winchester in the Supernatural TV Series, etc.

    Red Hood is a mysterious vigilante who will kill criminals unlike Batman and is mysterious and has a grudge against the Joker. It would be revealed to be Jason Todd, the Second Robin, Batman found Young Jason Todd trying to steal one of the Batmobile's Tires, then Batman decided to recruit Jason who would become the Second Robin. Jason as the Second Robin would be kidnapped, tortured, and then bombed by the Joker. He would then be resurrected by the Lazarus Pit and is mad at Bruce for not avenging him and Killing the Joker because of his moral to not Kill.
    Joker (John DiMaggio)
    John DiMaggio was born on September 4, 1968.

    Known for being an actor/voice actor and playing roles in The Super Mario Bros Movie as Uncle Arthur, Played as Frank Finnerty in the TV Series, Perry Mason, etc.

    Joker is one of the most well known and popular rogue's gallery of the Batman and is a criminal, a psychopath, and a lunatic. Many things can be said about him. One of them, is his origin on how he came to be, there are many various ones about his origin, but the common one, was where he was robbing things in ACE Chemicals, but was stopped by Batman, and out of fear, he accidentally fell off into a large tank/tube of chemicals, which made him insane and become the Joker, hence his famous Quote of "All it takes is one day."
    Dick Grayson (Neil Pattrick Haris)
    Neil Pattrick Haris was born on June 15, 1973.

    Known for being shown in TV Series and Movies like playing the role of Carl Jenkins in the movie Starship Troopers, played as Barney Stinson in How I met your Mother, etc.

    Dick Grayson, him and his parents were called the "Flying Graysons" for their skilled at trapeze after his parents died in a circus while performing by an "accident" which Tony Zucco sabotaged their trapeze equipment, which led to Dick's parents death and watched them die, Dick was then adopted by Bruce Wayne because he witnessed Dick's Parents die himself with his own two eyes and felt to adopt Dick because his situation was similar to his and was trained to become the first Robin. Dick would then give up his mantle after a few years and become under the identity/alias of Nightwing.
